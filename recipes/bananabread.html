Awesome Banana Bread from old colleague

Ingredients

  • 4 ripe mashed bananas
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/3 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 1/4 cups white sugar
  • 1/2 cup dark brown sugar
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • 1 3/4 cups flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 325 F
  2. Spray pans: 4 small loaf pans or 1 regular loaf pan
  3. Blend together bananas, eggs, oil and vanilla with a mixer at low speed
  4. Add in sugars and fully incorporate
  5. Add cinnamon, flour, baking soda and salt. Mix until just combined.
  6. Pour into prepared pan(s) and bake until toothpick inserted comes out clean:
    • small pans: about 40 minutes
    • 1 regular loaf pan: about 1 hour and 10 minutes. Start checking at 50 minutes.
